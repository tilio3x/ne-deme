# Privacy Policy - Ne Deme

**Last updated: April 19, 2026**

## Introduction

Ne Deme ("we", "our", "us") operates the Ne Deme mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Account type (Client or Tradesperson)

### Tradesperson Profile Information
If you register as a tradesperson, we additionally collect:
- Service categories offered
- Service description
- City
- Hourly rate (optional)
- Location (optional, with your permission)

### Location Data
With your explicit permission, we collect your device location to:
- Show nearby tradespeople in search results
- Calculate distances between clients and tradespeople
- Allow tradespeople to set their service area

You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings.

### Booking Information
When you request a service, we collect:
- Service description
- Requested date
- Communication between client and tradesperson

### Device Information
We collect:
- Firebase Cloud Messaging token (for push notifications)
- Device type and operating system version

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Connect clients with tradespeople
- Send push notifications about booking requests and updates
- Display search results and tradesperson profiles
- Calculate distances for location-based search
- Improve our services

## Information Sharing

We share your information only in these cases:
- **Between clients and tradespeople**: When a booking request is made, the client's name and service description are shared with the tradesperson
- **Tradesperson profiles**: Tradesperson profile information (name, categories, city, rating) is visible to all authenticated users
- **Service providers**: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and notifications

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, which provides:
- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Access controls via Firestore Security Rules
- Data center security compliant with SOC 1, SOC 2, and ISO 27001

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Update** your profile information through the app
- **Delete** your account by contacting us
- **Withdraw consent** for location data collection via device settings

## Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

Ne Deme is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
- Email: privacy@nedeme.com
